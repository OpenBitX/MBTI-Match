### 1. Global UI Style Guide & Mood Board (Design System)

**Prompt:**
Develop a comprehensive UI Style Guide and mood board for a "MBTI Love Personality Matching Test" web application. The visual style must be highly artistic, romantic, dreamy, and carry a sense of psychological mystery. Adopt a minimalist approach (remove Glassmorphism). The color palette should be dominated by soft, warm Love Pink and clean White, creating a gentle and inviting atmosphere. It must include an elegant typography hierarchy (serif fonts for titles), rounded capsule-shaped buttons, and clean, minimal input fields with subtle soft shadows (instead of glass effects). The layout should represent top-tier UI/UX design found on Dribbble, with ample white space and a harmonious, clean composition.

### 2. Home / MBTI Input Page (Landing & Input Page)

**Prompt:**
A web UI design of the landing and MBTI input page for the love matching test. The background features a dynamic, soft gradient of Love Pink transitioning into clean White, resembling a gentle light. Centered on the page are two elegantly designed input fields or dropdown menus for "My MBTI" and "Partner's MBTI" (replacing translucent frosted glass texture with clean, solid or subtly defined minimal fields). Large, artistic typography above them reads "The Collision of Souls." Below the inputs, a prominent "Begin Soul Resonance" button with a gentle glow effect is placed. The overall layout is centered, minimalist, with abundant breathing room, creating a romantic and poetic feeling.

### 3. Questionnaire Scenario Page (Questionnaire Page)

**Prompt:**
A web UI design of the questionnaire page for the MBTI love test. At the top of the page is an elegant, slender progress bar. In the center, a scenario-based question is displayed with clear, easily readable typography. Below are three answer options, designed as wide, minimal cards (replacing glassmorphism cards). When unselected, they are simple and clean; when selected, they feature a soft, subtle pink glowing border. In the background or adjacent to the question, abstract, minimalist geometric art forms or soft light halos appear. The overall style maintains a consistent soft Pink and White tone, creating a clean, modern, and inviting atmosphere.

### 4. Result Page (Result Page)

**Prompt:**
A web UI design of the result display page for the MBTI love matching test. In the center of the page, a highly artistic, super large serif font displays the matching percentage score (e.g., 98%). The score is surrounded by delicate starlight or subtle particle glow effects. Below the score is an elegantly typeset section of "artistic interpretation text," presented within a card defined by a soft Love Pink gradient border. At the bottom are two rounded capsule-shaped buttons: "Share Our Soul Pact" and "Retest." The background lighting is focused on the central score, with a gentle spotlight effect and subtle vignetting towards the edges (transitioning to softer pinks/whites), creating a strong sense of ritual.
